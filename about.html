About:
FeastFund was founded by Jack Botein, a high school student from Boston, MA to support local hunger prevention efforts during the COVID-19 Pandemic.
COVID-19 → 102% increase in MA Food Insecurity
Source: Project Bread
Our platform offers takeout 'feasts' from local Boston Restaurants, with each order funding a healthy meal donation to the Greater Boston Food Bank for a person in need.
1x FeastFund Meal → 1 Meal for a person in need
Simply pick your meal and pickup time on our website, and voilà you have supported the Greater Boston Food Bank's work to end hunger in Greater Boston.
